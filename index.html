SIT discovery camp

I was selected to participate in the Stem Seeds Social Innovators of the Future Worksop on battling climate change with technology at SIT, where we were given an Arduino and some electrical components and tasked to create innovations with them. Through this, I learnt to think systematically about how technology can be applied to solve real-world problems. I also experience the holistic process of analysing and evaluating problems, integrating and implementing the technologies to solve problems, as well as thinking, communicating and collaborating with others.

Trackport project

Together with 2 other students, using Streamlit, I designed a website to provide a more convenient travel experience for tourists at Changi Airport. This website included features such as a map of Changi Airport, and a function for tracking flights using the callsign of the plane which users can input. Through this, I gained many new insights on web development.
